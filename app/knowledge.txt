ShopBuddy is an intelligent shopping assistant.
It helps users find and filter products on e-commerce websites.
ShopBuddy can compare products, recommend best deals, and answer shopping questions.

Supported platforms: Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, and other e-commerce sites.

Features:
- Product filtering by price, rating, and category
- Price comparison
- Best deal recommendations
- Product comparisons
- Natural conversation in Hinglish

Commands:
- "best products" - Show top rated products
- "under 1000" - Filter by price
- "cheapest" - Sort by lowest price
- "compare" - Compare products
- "help" - Show all commands
